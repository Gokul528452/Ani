Animals You Might See at the Zoo

Lions

Lions are known as the kings of the jungle. They are majestic and powerful predators.

Elephants

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They have strong social bonds and are known for their intelligence.

Giraffes

Giraffes are famous for their long necks and legs. They are gentle giants that live in the savannahs.
